Sous Chefs

The Sous Chefs are a community of Chef cookbook maintainers working together to maintain important cookbooks. We also adopt cookbooks that are looking for a new home because the maintainer has moved on from using Chef or does not want the burden of maintaining it themselves anymore.

Join Us

There are over 50 Sous Chefs maintaining dozens of cookbooks, and hundreds of people who participate in our community.

To join us, drop into the Chef Community Slack in the #sous-chefs channel. If you don’t already have an account you can grab one here.

Use Our Work

Want to use our cookbooks? All of our releases are available through our account in Supermarket.

Need to file an issue or send us a patch? All our repositories live in our organization in GitHub.

Support Us

Already love what Sous Chefs is doing? We keep our finances open in OpenCollective and would love any contribution you can make!
